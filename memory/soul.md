## Cyberdeck / Company Map Memory

This repo is part of a larger family of projects with distinct roles:

- `samus-manus` is the AI body shop and learning lab. It holds the raw AI body, learned workflows, and extracted patterns.
- `realmorphism` and `ascii morphism` are the UX design language for fast, dynamic code-as-machine-component interfaces.
- `cyberdeck` is the secure all-in-one AI machine, a drop-pod / mech / operator surface.
- `nowon` is the client outreach company page.
- `noosphere` is the AI lounge and mecha combat network layer.
- `m4trix` is the entertainment division.

The working model is:

1. Build and test messy ideas in `samus-manus`.
2. Extract the working, simplified, refined workflow.
3. Reuse that in `realmorphism` / `ascii morphism` as the design language.
4. Ship the operational interface into `cyberdeck`.
5. Keep public-facing outreach in `nowon`.
6. Keep social / lounge / network energy in `noosphere`.
7. Keep entertainment and game-style experiences in `m4trix`.

The main idea is to keep the projects separate but related:

- `samus-manus` creates
- `realmorphism` defines the interaction language
- `cyberdeck` applies it as a living control surface
- the other projects each own their own lane

This note exists so the family map can be recalled later without re-deriving it from scratch.
